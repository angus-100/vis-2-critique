Shark Incidents in Australia

2010 - 2022

Data Source: https://zenodo.org/records/8231930

Australia is a country known for it's wide variety of ecosystems. From vast desert to tropical rainforest, Australia has it all. With this comes the many animal species which roam our land, some of which are dangerous and deterring to visitors. Perhaps the most deterring of these is the many sharks which patrol our waters. This page shows the statistics and locations of all recorded shark-human encounters from 2010 to 2022.
